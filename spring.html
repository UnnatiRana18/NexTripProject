Explore Spring Destinations in India

Experience the vibrant blooms and refreshing beauty of India during spring with these top destinations!

Valley of Flowers, Uttarakhand

Budget: ₹10,000 - ₹15,000 per person

Activities: Trekking, Nature Photography, Wildlife Exploration

The Valley of Flowers is a UNESCO World Heritage site that comes alive in spring with its vibrant meadows and diverse flora.

Nainital, Uttarakhand

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹12,000 per person

Activities: Boating, Nature Walks, Sightseeing

Nainital, with its serene lakes and blooming surroundings, is a perfect springtime retreat.

Gangtok, Sikkim

Budget: ₹9,000 - ₹14,000 per person

Activities: Monastery Visits, Cable Car Rides, Exploring Orchids

Gangtok offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas and vibrant orchards in full bloom during spring.

Munnar, Kerala

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹11,000 per person

Activities: Tea Garden Walks, Waterfalls, Wildlife Spotting

Munnar is a haven in spring with its lush tea plantations, cascading waterfalls, and refreshing climate.
