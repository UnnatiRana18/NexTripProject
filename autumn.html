Explore Autumn Destinations in India

Experience the vibrant colors and serene beauty of India during autumn with these top destinations!

Kashmir

Budget: ₹12,000 - ₹18,000 per person

Activities: Shikara Rides, Houseboats, Apple Orchards

Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is breathtaking in autumn with its golden Chinar trees, serene lakes, and charming houseboats.

Darjeeling

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹12,000 per person

Activities: Tea Garden Walks, Toy Train Rides, Trekking

Known for its lush tea gardens and stunning views of the Kanchenjunga, Darjeeling is a serene retreat during the autumn season.

Udaipur

Budget: ₹10,000 - ₹15,000 per person

Activities: Lake Rides, Palace Visits, Cultural Tours

Known as the "City of Lakes," Udaipur is enchanting in autumn with its romantic palaces, tranquil lakes, and vibrant festivals.

Coorg

Budget: ₹6,000 - ₹10,000 per person

Activities: Coffee Plantation Tours, Nature Walks, Waterfalls

Coorg, known as the "Scotland of India," is a perfect autumn escape with its lush coffee plantations, misty hills, and cascading waterfalls.
