Explore Summer Destinations in India

Discover the best places to visit in India this summer with exciting activities and affordable options!

Goa

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹12,000 per person

Activities: Beach Parties, Water Sports, Sightseeing

Goa is the perfect summer getaway with its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and a wide variety of water sports like parasailing and jet-skiing.

Kerala

Budget: ₹10,000 - ₹15,000 per person

Activities: Houseboat Rides, Ayurveda, Backwater Exploration

Known as "God's Own Country", Kerala offers a refreshing escape with its backwaters, houseboats, and rejuvenating Ayurvedic treatments.

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Budget: ₹6,000 - ₹10,000 per person

Activities: River Rafting, Yoga, Trekking

Rishikesh is a hub for adventure sports and spiritual retreats. River rafting on the Ganges, yoga, and treks make it a thrilling summer destination.

Andaman Islands

Budget: ₹15,000 - ₹20,000 per person

Activities: Snorkeling, Scuba Diving, Island Hopping

The Andaman Islands are a tropical paradise offering clear waters for snorkeling, scuba diving, and island hopping with stunning marine life and pristine beaches.
