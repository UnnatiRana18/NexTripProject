Explore Summer Destinations in India

Find your perfect summer getaway with budget-friendly options and exciting activities!

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Budget: ₹10,000 - ₹15,000 per person

Activities: Trekking, Paragliding, River Rafting

Manali is a picturesque hill station surrounded by the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas. It's perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹12,000 per person

Activities: Tea Garden Tours, Toy Train Ride, Hiking

Known for its lush tea gardens and panoramic views of Mount Kanchenjunga, Darjeeling is a serene summer retreat.

Ooty, Tamil Nadu

Budget: ₹9,000 - ₹14,000 per person

Activities: Boating, Botanical Garden Visits, Scenic Train Rides

Ooty is a charming hill station in South India, offering a cool escape with its beautiful lakes and gardens.

Leh-Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir

Budget: ₹15,000 - ₹20,000 per person

Activities: Biking, Monastery Tours, Pangong Lake Visit

Leh-Ladakh is a high-altitude desert known for its breathtaking landscapes, monasteries, and thrilling adventure activities.
