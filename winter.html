Explore Winter Destinations in India

Find your perfect winter getaway with budget-friendly options and exciting activities!

Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Budget: ₹8,000 - ₹12,000 per person

Activities: Skiing, Snow Trekking, Ice Skating

Shimla is a perfect winter retreat known for its snowfall, colonial architecture, and vibrant markets. It’s a great place for a winter holiday.

Gulmarg, Jammu & Kashmir

Budget: ₹12,000 - ₹18,000 per person

Activities: Skiing, Gondola Ride, Snowboarding

Gulmarg is famous for its snowy landscapes and is known as the "Meadow of Flowers." It's a paradise for snow sports enthusiasts.

Leh-Ladakh, Jammu & Kashmir

Budget: ₹15,000 - ₹25,000 per person

Activities: Snow Trekking, Ice Climbing, Monastery Tours

Leh-Ladakh in winter is a wonderland with frozen lakes and snow-covered mountains. It's ideal for those seeking a unique and adventurous winter experience.

Nainital, Uttarakhand

Budget: ₹7,000 - ₹10,000 per person

Activities: Boating, Snow-view Point, Visit Naina Devi Temple

Nainital is a picturesque hill station that transforms into a snowy paradise during the winter. It offers a mix of adventure and tranquility.
